Welcome to [MYST] The Mystic Tribute!

Hello and Welcome to the guild! We are a Guild Wars 2 guild established in 2020. We are all about mystic tributes here! Many times players in Guild Wars 2 get stuck on making their mystic tributes. It's hard stuff we get it! We are here to help you!

Here are some of the things that we do as a guild. From Open World, Strikes & Raids, to even World vs. World there will always be a spot for you! Lets get you those Mystic Tributes!

  • Run a variety of event trains ranging from all game modes
  • Help train people for strikes and raids
  • Weekly giveaways including legendary weapons
  • Do Guild missions every Sunday at reset
  • Help people with building there legendary weapons

Important Info for joining Events!

Hey adventurer! We are really happy you would like to join us on our events. We do have a couple things to know when it comes to our events so please read them.

  1. Be kind to each other. We are all human we all make mistakes sometimes!
  2. Try your best to follow the commander of your squad.
  3. If you are doing raids or strikes, try to have a decent build.
  4. The most important rule: Have fun!

Looking to Join? Great!

Looking for a new guild? Looking for people to do stuff with? Looking for a guild who will help you with your legendary? We are here for you!

How to Join

There are a few way how you can join us. Any will work!

  1. Send an in-game mail to Cynstrider.9145 saying you want to join the guild, with what gamemodes you are interested in, and your favorite looking legendary.
  2. Send an email to mystictributeguildwars2@gmail.com with a title saying you want to join the guild. Put in your Guild Wars 2 username, let us know what gamemodes you are interested in, and your favorite looking legendary.
  3. Join our guild's Discord server and send a message in #guild-invites. Have it include your Guild Wars 2 username, gamemodes you are interested in, and your favorite looking legendary.
